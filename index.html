Joshua Wilson is a New York based software engineer. In 2016 he joined the Data team at Enigma, where he built ETL pipelines for fortune 50 enterprises. His role gradually shifted to focus on leadership, mentoring, and planning for a new team called “Envoy”. This team is responsible for delivering Enigma’s products and building data-intensive applications across extremely diverse IT landscapes.

In 2014 he founded DoubleDoss Labs, an independent consultancy focusing on minimum viable product development for startups. He’s built web, iOS, and Android applications, developed a ranking algorithm, and modeled financial data.

From 2011 to 2015 Josh worked at Hudson River Trading, where he built global trading and risk systems and worked on a variety of projects ranging from data visualizations to backend tooling to devops.

Josh holds a M.S. in Physics from the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill, NC. There he researched computational and analytical solutions to nonlinear PDEs and, more generally, scientific computing. Josh also holds a B.A. in Physics and Computer Science from Hendrix College in Conway, AR.

Josh grew up in a house full of tools, ever-changing hobbies, and DIY attitudes that shaped how he approaches his work today.
